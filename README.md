# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** April 27, 2026

This application Xpand is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our App.

---

## 1. Camera Permission

Our App requests access to your device camera solely to support core features, such as:

* Scanning QR codes or barcodes
* Capturing images for transaction or operational purposes

The camera is only accessed when you actively use these features. The App does not access the camera in the background.

---

## 2. Data Collection and Usage

* The App does **not automatically collect or store** images or video captured using the camera.
* Any data captured is used only for the intended feature (e.g., scanning codes for transactions).
* We do not use camera data for advertising or tracking purposes.

---

## 3. Data Sharing

We do **not sell, rent, or share** your data with third parties.

We may disclose information only if required to do so by law or in response to valid legal requests.

---

## 4. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure.

---

## 5. User Control

You can control camera access at any time through your device settings.
Disabling camera permission may limit certain features of the App.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised "Last updated" date.
